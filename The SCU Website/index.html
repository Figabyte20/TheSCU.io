Welcome to the company, Zero Walker.

The SCU is a turn based RPG set in the land of Tarastara. Home to the Points of Humanity, these wells of infinite energy bring life and growth to all around them. Sometimes, however, these points can corrupt and flip their output, turning anybody trapped into twisted Shades with one goal: consume all. The SCU is in charge of protecting the population from this threat, but what happens when the ones who swore to protect you use their greatest enemy for their own selfish gain?

Game Features

Unique Party Members

Lead your team of Zero Walkers to protect the people from the growing tyrrany and grow as a unit.

Challenging Battles

Defeat enemies in tough turn based combat that rewards good reaction time and strategy.

Vast Augment System

Equip each party member with a variety of Augments to turn a healer into a damage machine and the leader into the ultimate support.
