About the Game

The SCU is a turn-based RPG set in the land of Tarastara. Home to the Points of Humanity, these wells of infinite energy bring life and growth to all around them.


Sometimes, however, these points can corrupt and flip their output, turning anybody trapped into twisted Shades with one goal: to consume all.


The SCU is in charge of protecting the population from this threat, but what happens when the ones who swore to protect you use their greatest enemy for their own selfish gain?
