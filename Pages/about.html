The Land of Tarastara

The SCU takes place in Tarastara, the only region of the world with an active Zero Point. This source of energy, known as Flux, gives life to the land and allows civilization to grow around powerful leaks called the Points of Humanity.


But when a Zero Leak corrupts, its energy reverses and begins infecting living creatures. Those caught in the corruption become Shades: warped, dangerous beings that exist only to consume.


The SCU was created to protect people from these outbreaks. But as the company grows more powerful, its leader begin using the very threat they swore to stop.

Stand Out Features

Dynamic Story

The route you go down will determine both yours and the fate of the people of this land.

Engaging Turn-Based Gameplay

No longer do you simply wait for the enemy to take their turn! See who the enemy is targeting and plan your actions accordingly, then Quick Guard to avoid damage!

No Single-Best-Strategy

Designed with niche playstyles in mind, there is no one best party build. Everything from "the healer just screen wiped the enemies" to "they died to debuffs in 3 turns" is a viable way to play!

Flux-Powered World

The world of Tarastara is powered by Flux, a life force flowing from Zero Points. These points allow towns, plants, animals, and people to survive.

The Points of Humanity

Tarastara is home to the Points of Humanity: powerful Flux leaks that allow towns and societies to grow in different ways.

These points include Order, Wealth, Forgery, Knowledge, Culture, Health, and Abundance.

Corrupted Shades

When corrupt Flux infects living creatures, they become Shades: twisted monsters rooted to the source of their corruption.

Unlocking

In desperate moments, some people can connect directly to Flux. This power can turn the tide of battle, but using it comes with serious consequences.
